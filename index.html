LinkBoard

Mastering Your Digital Workspace with LinkBoard

LinkBoard is a professional-grade bookmark management utility designed to streamline your web navigation. Unlike traditional browser bookmarks that get buried in menus, LinkBoard provides a visual, group-based dashboard that allows for instant access to your most critical resources.

Why Use Visual Grouping?

Research shows that visual spatial recognition is faster than reading text lists. By assigning custom icons and grouping links into logical categories like "Work," "Social," or "Research," users can reduce cognitive load and improve daily productivity. Our board uses 128px high-definition favicons to ensure every link is instantly recognizable.

Privacy & Data Sovereignty

One of the core pillars of LinkBoard is data privacy. This tool operates entirely on Local Storage. This means your URLs, group names, and layout configurations are never transmitted to a cloud server. You have total control over your data through our Backup and Restore system, which allows you to export your entire configuration as a JSON file for local safekeeping.

Pro Tips for LinkBoard Users

  • Tile Position: Use the move buttons to prioritize your most-used links to the top-left of each group.
  • Timestamped Backups: We recommend creating a backup once a week to ensure you never lose your organized workspace.
  • Cross-Browser Portability: Since LinkBoard is a static utility, you can take your JSON backup and restore it on any device running a modern browser.
