Effective Date: January 2024

Welcome to LinkBoard. Your privacy is critically important to us. Because this tool operates as a client-side utility, we have designed our data practices to be as transparent as possible.

LinkBoard is a "Privacy-First" application. All data you enter—including URLs, group names, and custom images—is stored exclusively in your browser's Local Storage. Like most standard website servers, we may collect basic log information (such as IP addresses and browser types) for security and analytics purposes. This data is not linked to any personally identifiable information.
